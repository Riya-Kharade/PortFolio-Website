ABOUT ME

My name is Riya Sunil Kharade, and I am from the city of Khanapur. I recently turned 18 on March 16, 2023. I have completed my matriculation with an impressive score of 95.40% from the Maharashtra State Board of Higher Secondary Education. at the esteemed Mahatma Gandhi Vidyalaya in Khanapur.

Being passionate about technology, I decided to pursue my education in the field of computers. I enrolled in a Government Polytechnic college in Karad to obtain a computer diploma. My dedication and enthusiasm for this field have been evident throughout my academic journey.

I have always had a strong inclination towards staying updated with the latest advancements and innovations in the world of technology. This drive has motivated me to expand my knowledge and skills in programming. I am proficient in languages like C, C++, and Java.Aside from my technical skills, I am also a multilingual individual. I can fluently speak Marathi, Hindi, and English. This ability allows me to effectively communicate and connect with people from diverse backgrounds.

I come from a close-knit family consisting of my loving mother and supportive sister. I am extremely grateful for the encouragement and guidance they have provided, which has shaped me into a driven and motivated individual.
